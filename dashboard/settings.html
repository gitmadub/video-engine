      Own adverts

      Manage custom VAST and popup ad placements for your player embeds.

      Premium Bandwidth is required to place your own ads on the video player.

      Tracking tokens for Vast & Popup ads

      [domain] The domain name the player is embedded on.
      [player-height] The height of the video player on the page in pixels.
      [player-width] The width of the video player on the page in pixels.
      [random] Typically used as a cache-buster, to ensure there is no local caching of the VAST response.
      [page-url] The URL of the current page (the one the video is embedded in).
      [timestamp] The current time of the user's computer, as a UNIX timestamp.
      [referrer] The URL of the page that brought the viewer to the current player.
